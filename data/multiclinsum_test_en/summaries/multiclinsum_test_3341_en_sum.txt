We report a case of intracerebral hemorrhage following vaccination with ChAdOx1-S. A middle-aged female patient with no medical history of interest presented to the emergency department 16 days after the first dose of ChAdOx1-S with a sudden onset left-sided hemiplegia and severe occipital headaches. She had not received heparin in the previous 100 days. Blood work showed moderate thrombocytopenia and computed tomography showed a right frontal lobar hemorrhage without thrombosis on computed tomography venography. Antibodies to platelet factor 4 were confirmed in the blood. The patient developed a treatment-resistant intracranial hypertension syndrome and died three weeks later.
