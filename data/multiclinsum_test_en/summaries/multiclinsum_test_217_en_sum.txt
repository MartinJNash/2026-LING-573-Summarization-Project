We report an unusual case of a 72-year-old man with low-grade appendiceal mucinous neoplasm, which was found accidently during the investigation of the bladder tumor on a CT scan. Pathological examination of the specimen showed a cystic dilation at the distal tip of the appendix, filled with gelatinous mucus. Microscopically, we found mucinous epithelial proliferation with extracellular mucin.
