A thymoma case with autoimmune enteropathy and myocarditis undergoing surgery was reported. The symptoms and laboratory results of the patient had dramatically fluctuated after tumor resection and gradually alleviated. The whole exome sequencing found MDM4 amplified in tumor cells. Immunohistochemistry indicated that thymoma cells were positive for MDM4. The result of drug sensitivity tests showed thymoma cells were highly sensitive to Nutlin-3a.
