Herein, we report a case of a 71-year-old man with SCBC who underwent radical cystectomy and received adjuvant chemotherapy with gemcitabine and cisplatin. However, recurrent tumors were found 6 months postoperatively. The patient was then treated with carboplatin, etoposide, and atezolizumab and achieved complete response. He continues receiving maintenance therapy with atezolizumab monotherapy without any evidence of recurrence over the 12 months follow up.
