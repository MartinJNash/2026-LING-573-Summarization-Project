We report a 42-year-old heterosexual woman with an abscess of the Bartholin's gland caused by Neisseria meningitidis, which was isolated by culture and identified by VITEK®2. Molecular characterisation and subtyping was performed by sequencing the sequotipe and clonal complex. Antigenic profile was studied by amplification and sequencing of the genes encoding PorA, fHbp, NHBA and NadA and antimicrobial profile was studied by MIC. Molecular diagnosis allows to confirm N. meningitidis as the causative agent of bartholinitis.
