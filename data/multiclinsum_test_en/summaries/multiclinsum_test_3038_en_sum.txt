We report a case of a 15-year-old patient presenting with progressive horizontal diplopia first at distance and eventually at near for 2 years. He had a history of 6 hours of smartphone usage per day. Orthoptic examination showed esotropia of 35 prism diopters (PD) at distance and 30 PD at near. Neuroimaging was normal. The patient underwent bilateral medial recession of 5.5 mm. After 8 months of follow-up, the patient was orthophoric, and full stereopsis was restored.
