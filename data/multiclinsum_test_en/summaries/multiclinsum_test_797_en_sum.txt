A 37-year-old African man born in Sierra Leone was admitted. After admission, his condition rapidly worsened due to severe septic shock and progressive multiorgan failure. No neurological signs were seen. Despite treatment with antibiotics, fluid resuscitation, and norepinephrine, the multiorgan failure deteriorated further and he died as a result. Blood and synovial fluid cultures showed Clostridium botulinum. No botulinum toxins were found.
