We present a case of a 55-year-old male patient with a giant osteochondroma originating from the neck of the talus. The patient presented with a huge 100 × 70 × 50 mm swelling over the ankle. The patient underwent an excision of the swelling. Histopathological examination of the swelling confirmed the findings of an osteochondroma. The patient had an uneventful recovery after the excision and resumed his functional activity completely.
