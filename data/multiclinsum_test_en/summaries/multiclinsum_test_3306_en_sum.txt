Patient concerns: A 29-year-old female presented with infertility.

Diagnosis: IHH diagnosis was confirmed through magnetic resonance (MR) scan, endocrine tests, physical examination, and B ultrasonic inspection. Additionally, genetic studies, including chromosome analysis, were conducted for the patient. The results confirmed no genetic abnormalities or concerns.

Interventions: The patient underwent multiple ovulation induction programs.

Outcome: After several ovulation induction cycles, the patient conceived and delivered a live baby.
