This patient's HbA1c was in the pre-diabetic range (6.3%) 6 months prior to initiating teprotumumab. After three doses, the patient was hospitalized with hyperosmolar hyperglycemic nonketotic syndrome and an HbA1c of 11.7%. He was diagnosed with type 2 diabetes mellitus and treated with insulin aspart mixed 70/30. He remained on this regimen for 14 months with an A1c of 6.0%. He then self-discontinued the insulin, with an A1c 4 months later measuring 5.5%. The patient's latest HbA1c approximately two and a half years after hospitalization was 6.1% on no medications.
