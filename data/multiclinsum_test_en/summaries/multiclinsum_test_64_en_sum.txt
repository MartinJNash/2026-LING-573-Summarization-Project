A 68 years male presented with a gradually enlarging scrotal mass over a period of 6month duration. On examination a mass was felt (5 cm × 4 cm) occupying left side of scrotum and abutting the lower pole of left testis. Left inguinal orchidectomy along with excision of mass was done. HPE revealed a spindle cell pattern with low cellularity and Immunohistochemical analysis revealed the tumor reactive for CD 117 and CD 34 which is suggestive of GIST.
