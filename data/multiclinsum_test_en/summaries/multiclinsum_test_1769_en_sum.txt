This girl presented after birth severe contractures of legs. She was admitted at 30 days of age with poor feeding, cholestatic jaundice with normal GGT and failure to thrive . Also we have noted a severe acidosis (pH=7.2) associated with aminoaciduria and glucosuria. At second month of age the girl presented a severe ichtyosis, recurrent fever and constipation. Apart from treatment the constipation has persisted. The baby died of sepsis at 12 weeks of age.
