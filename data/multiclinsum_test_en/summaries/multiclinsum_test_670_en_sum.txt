A 79-year-old woman visited our hospital because of lower abdominal discomfort. She was endoscopically diagnosed with type 0-IIa + IIc cancer of the cecum, and biopsy of the lesion showed signet-ring cell carcinoma. With a preoperative clinical staging of cStage I (cT2, cN0, cM0), the patient underwent laparoscopic ileocecal resection with D3 lymphadenectomy. Histopathological examination of the resected specimens revealed signet-ring cell carcinoma
