Here, a 28-year-old man presented with a right preauricular swelling for 9 months. Ultrasound of the head region - and of the peak and fine cytology aspirate Diagnosis of neoplasm in the parotid gland, most likely a benign tumour. After that, a total parotidectomy with preservation of the facial nerve was performed. On the histopathological and immunohistochemical study, was consistent with the diagnosis of ACC in the parotid gland.
