Male with no family or perinatal history of interest with neurodevelopmental impairment: psychomotor retardation, language delay and cognitive impairment, macrocephaly (p > 99) and obesity. Facial dysmorphic phenotype: wide forehead, deep set eyes, bulbous nose, prominent auricles and sharp chin. Array CGH deletion of 586 kb in Xq21.1, including a single gene associated with the disease, BRWD3. Subsequently, the mother and sister, asymptomatic, were studied and both carry the deletion.
