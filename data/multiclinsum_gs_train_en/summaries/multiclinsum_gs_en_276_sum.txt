We present a 11-year-old boy with orthostatic headaches and vomiting for 12 hours after a fall on the sacrococcygeal region; a history of Marfan syndrome. Cranio-spinal MRI showed extradural fluid collections at the dorsal and lumbosacral level consistent with CSF fistula. The condition resolved after treatment, but there were two new episodes during follow-up, so a hematic epidural patch was applied two years after the onset.
