79-year-old female patient, who developed a fast-growing necrotic tumour after a cut to the fifth finger of her left hand. Ultrasound and angiography suggested a haematoma. Surgical treatment was decided, during which it was observed that the tumour involved the ulnar collateral digital artery of the fifth finger. The lesion and the involved arterial segment were resected. She had a post-surgical course without complications. The histopathological diagnosis of pseudoaneurysm of the lesion was confirmed.
