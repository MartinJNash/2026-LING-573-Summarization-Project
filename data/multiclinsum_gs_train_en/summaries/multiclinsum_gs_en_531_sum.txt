We present a case of hepatoonfalocele with prenatal diagnosis, without associated anomalies. In week 37 under maternal and fetal sedation, botulinum toxin was injected into the right hemiabdomen. After the scheduled cesarean section in week 39, injection into the left hemiabdomen was completed and a complete reduction of the hepatic content and total inversion of the amnion was achieved. Definitive repair was performed with separation of components on the eighth day of life and discharge on the fourteenth day of life.
