We present the case of a 69-year-old patient with uncontrolled diabetes mellitus, who came to the emergency department with a 3-day history of right molar pain with subsequent right facial swelling and periorbital cellulitis. The case showed a cyclical evolution influenced by the existing comorbidities, so the patient was subjected to both surgical and medical treatment by a multidisciplinary team. In addition, we performed a literature review on FN.
