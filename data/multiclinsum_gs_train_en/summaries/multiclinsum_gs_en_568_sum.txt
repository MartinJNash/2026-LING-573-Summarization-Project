18-year-old male patient with a diagnosis of NF-1 since childhood who presented with multiple oval nodules on the face, occipital region and wrist, red-blue macules on the back and a pink atrophic plaque on the thigh. The ultrasound study of the nodules was compatible with neurofibromas and the histopathological study of the lesions on the back and thigh was compatible with cutaneous neurofibromas.
