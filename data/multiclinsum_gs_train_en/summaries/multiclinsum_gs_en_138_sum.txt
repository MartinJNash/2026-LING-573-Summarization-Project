Female, 10 months old with pre-existing chronic disease, admitted since birth, used mechanical ventilation via tracheostomy, evolved with hypoxemia, need for oxygen therapy and increased ventilatory parameters, and COVID-19 was confirmed. Bronchial clearance techniques and maintenance of pulmonary expansion were performed. In addition, the child during admission acquired cephalic control, sitting without support, rolling and reaching for objects, and during coronavirus infection, guidelines were passed to the family for maintenance of acquired motor milestones.
