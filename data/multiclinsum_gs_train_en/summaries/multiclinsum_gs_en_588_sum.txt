We describe an iatrogenic aortic root dissection and contained aortic laceration during TAVI procedure with a 29-mm Evolut FX in an 88-year-old man with a history of coronary artery bypasses. Postprocedural computed tomography revealed a dissection in the noncoronary sinus of Valsalva with a mediastinal hematoma, evolving into a well defined pseudoaneurysm (20 mm). The patient received conservative management, resulting in a favorable clinical outcome.
