We aim to present the first case in literature, according to our knowledge, of both tubulocystic renal cell carcinoma and probable adenomatoid tumor existing at the same time in a 67-year-old Lebanese male presenting with epididymo-orchitis, who on ultrasound was found to have an incidental finding of probable adenomatoid tumor located in the tail of the right epididymis, as suggested by the radiologist and the urology team. The patient was also found to have a renal mass that was described earlier to be a Bosniak type 2 renal cyst. The patient underwent left partial nephrectomy. A pathological examination revealed tubulocystic renal cell carcinoma.
