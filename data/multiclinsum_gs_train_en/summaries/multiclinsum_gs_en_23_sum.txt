The nine-year-old male patient put his life at risk by attempting suicide by hanging in the bathroom. He received emergency care and was in the intensive care unit. During his hospitalization he was approached by the mental health service. Among the factors that have been previously associated with suicide attempts in school age that could be identified in the patient are: family disintegration, symptoms of severe depressive episode, difficulty in learning to read and a disorder of attention deficit with hyperactivity. The patient received treatment and follow-up, and to date has not returned to suicidal behaviour. He is currently thirteen years old.
