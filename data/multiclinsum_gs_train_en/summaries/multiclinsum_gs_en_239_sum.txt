A 31-year-old Vietnamese female initially presented with seizures, severe hyponatremia, and hypertension after COVID-19. Despite the initial treatment, she had recurrent seizures and developed PRES as confirmed by magnetic resonance imaging. Further investigations revealed a genetic mutation of c.517 C > T in HMBS, leading to a diagnosis of AHP. Treatment with hemin significantly improved her symptoms and corrected her electrolyte imbalance.
