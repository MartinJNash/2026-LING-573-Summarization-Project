36-year-old man with a history of HIV diagnosed in 2017, on antiretroviral treatment and a history of Kaposi's sarcoma. He began his condition in March 2021 with pain in the lower back, accompanied by weakness and anesthesia in the pelvic limbs, with bladder incontinence and abdominal distension. A complete medullary syndrome with a level at T8-T9 was integrated into the exploration, corroborating a longitudinally extensive lesion from T8-L1 in the MRI, with CSF with proteinohraquia and seropositivity to AQP4. A diagnosis of NMO was integrated by Wingerchuk criteria and treatment with rituximab was initiated with symptomatic improvement, objectified with an expanded scale of the state (EDSS) of 4 to 1.
