51-year-old male patient with a history of gouty arthritis; who has a clinical picture of approximately four years of evolution, characterized by pain in the right hip of intensity 8/10 on a pain analogue scale, without irradiation, associated with functional limitation characterized by reduction of mobility arcs and impossibility of standing. Imaging studies are performed which are suggestive of a tumour lesion at the level of the proximal femur with characteristics of malignancy, for which a biopsy and subsequent histopathological diagnosis of gouty tophi are performed.
