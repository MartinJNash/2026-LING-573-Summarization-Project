We present the case of an 82-year-old male patient with a newly diagnosed moderately differentiated colon adenocarcinoma and a hypermetabolic periaortic nodule as an incidental finding.

Results: Percutaneous biopsy of the periaortic node confirmed the diagnosis of schwannoma. At one year follow-up, the schwannoma has demonstrated growth. There is no evidence of progression of his cancer disease.
