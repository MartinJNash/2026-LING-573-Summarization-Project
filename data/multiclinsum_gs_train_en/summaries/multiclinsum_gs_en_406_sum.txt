We report a case of BK virus nephropathy in a patient who underwent heart transplantation due to peripartum cardiomyopathy. The renal biopsy reported active chronic tubulointerstitial nephritis associated with late-stage BK virus nephritis and the blood viral load for BK virus was positive (log 4.5). The immunosuppressive treatment was reduced, and after two years of follow-up, the patient had stable renal function with serum creatinine of 2.5 mg/dL (GFR of 23.4 mL/min/1.73m2).
