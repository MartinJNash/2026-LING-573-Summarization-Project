We describe the transjugular closure of a residual atrial septal defect with significant left-to-right shunt due to a malpositioned large atrial septal device in a symptomatic 74-year-old female. Transjugular access was chosen first due to the unfavourable position of the device for delivery of a new one from the femoral approach. An overlapping Figulla® Flex II 27/30 mm PFO device was successfully implanted with the guidance of 3D-transoesophageal echocardiography.
