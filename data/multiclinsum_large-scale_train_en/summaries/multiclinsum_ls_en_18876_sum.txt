A case of preterm neonate who presented immediately after delivery with hyperglycemia and heart murmur. Clinical and laboratory investigation showed diabetes mellitus type I and double outlet right ventricle. Genetic examination showed classic mutations in the EIF2AK3 gene - eukaryotic translation initiation factor 2α kinase 3. Conclusion: Diabetes in neonatal age raises doubts about the possibility of association with the syndrome and other diseases.
