A patient with multiple unruptured intracranial aneurysms underwent staged treatment. A large basilar apex aneurysm was treated with a flow-diverter stent combined with a flow-T stenting-assisted coiling technique in the first stage, and a giant supraclinoid aneurysm was treated with a flow-diverter stent applied in the second stage. Clinical presentations, technical details, intra- and perioperative complications, and clinical and angiographic outcomes were recorded, with a 9-month follow-up.
