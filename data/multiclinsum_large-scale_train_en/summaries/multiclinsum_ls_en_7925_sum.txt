We present the case of a 68-year old woman who was admitted to the hospital due to fatigue and severe anemia (hemoglobin 65 g/l). Laboratory results also revealed high-grade thrombocytopenia (8 × 109/l). The abdominal ultrasound and computed tomography scan showed multiple lesions throughout the liver, spleen and kidneys. After the histological examination of the liver biopsy, the patient was diagnosed with hepatic angiosarcoma. The treatment with first-line chemotherapy (doxorubicin) was initiated despite ongoing paraneoplastic syndrome - disseminative intravascular coagulopathy. However, the disease was terminal, and the patient died 2 months since diagnosed.
