42-year-old pregnant lady at 24 weeks of gestation presented with classical symptoms of acute appendicitis. She had leukocytosis but the Ultrasound was equivocal. She underwent laparoscopic appendectomy and found to have an inflamed appendix. Postoperative recovery was satisfactory and was discharged home. The histopathology report showed low-grade mucinous neoplasm of the appendix and she was detailed about it on follow up.
