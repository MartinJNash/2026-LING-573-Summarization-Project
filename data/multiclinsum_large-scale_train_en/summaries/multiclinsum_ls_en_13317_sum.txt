A 71-year-old non-smoker Chinese female was diagnosed with advanced lung adenocarcinoma harboring EGFR ex20ins (N771delinsKG). The patient received first-line afatinib (40 mg/day) therapy and a significant and substantial reduction in tumor size was observed subsequently. According to RESIST 1.1, a radiological partial response was achieved. The final progression-free survival was 10 months.
