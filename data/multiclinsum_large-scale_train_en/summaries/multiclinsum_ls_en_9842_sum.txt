A 35-year-old male presented to our emergency department with a history of fall in ditch under influence of alcohol with swelling of her right elbow and deformity of right upper limb. Roentgenograms showed oblique fracture of the radial shaft and dislocation of radial head posteriorly. He underwent open reduction and internal fixation of fracture with limited contact dynamic compression plate and reduction of radial head and fixing with radiocapitellar wire. After 4 months, the fracture healed fully and he had complete full range of elbow movements.
