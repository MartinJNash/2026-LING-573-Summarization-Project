We report a case of PRES in a patient suffering from complicated diverticulitis. Following medical management in the emergency department, the patient deteriorated, becoming hypotensive and altered. Bowel resection under general anesthesia was performed. Postoperative brain MRI demonstrated bilateral and symmetric T2 signal hyperintensities suggestive of PRES. Following supportive treatment, the patient was discharged from the surgical intensive care unit on postoperative day 21 with no residual deficits.
