We report a case report of a 29 years old male who presented an occasional and asymptomatic mediastinal mass. Computer Tomography (CT) scan showed a 6.8 × 4.5 cm mass in the anterior mediastino, located below the left brachio-cephalic vein, next to aortic arch and left pulmonary artery. Complete excision of the mass "en block" with anterior mediastinal adipose tissue was achieved, using uniportal VATS approach. The postoperative course was regular, without air leak or other pulmonary complication.
