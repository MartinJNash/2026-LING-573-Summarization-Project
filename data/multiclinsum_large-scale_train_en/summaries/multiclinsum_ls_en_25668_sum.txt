Herein, we present a case of SFT in a 45 years old female. MR imaging showed isointense to hypointense signal intensity, and marked enhancement. Microscopically, the tumors showed characteristic "patternless-pattern" architecture. Elongated tumor cells formed fascicles alternating with hypocellular densely collagenous stroma. Immunohistochemistry for STAT6, CD34, BCL2 and CD99 favours the definitive diagnosis of SFT.
