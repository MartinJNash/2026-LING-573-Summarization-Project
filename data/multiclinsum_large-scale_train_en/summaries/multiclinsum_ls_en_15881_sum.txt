The case presented is a 28-year-old female patient who was not a known case of Polysplenia Syndrome. During the patients' pre-operative assessment, physical examination was done and a type of heterotaxy syndrome was suspected upon palpation of the stomach. Therefore, Computed Tomography (CT) imaging was performed which confirmed PSP. She underwent laparoscopic sleeve gastrectomy due to her morbid obesity, as her Body Mass Index (BMI) was 40.8 kg/m2. Post-operatively, water soluble contrast study was performed to rule out leak and the patient had uneventful follow-up.
