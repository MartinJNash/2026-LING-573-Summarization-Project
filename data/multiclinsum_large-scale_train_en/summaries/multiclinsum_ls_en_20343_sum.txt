We describe the case of a 77-year-old man who underwent re-salvage focal low-dose rate brachytherapy for local recurrence after carbon ion radiotherapy and salvage focal low-dose rate brachytherapy. We performed re-salvage focal low-dose rate brachytherapy for the recurrence with a different type of seed, which resulted in a significant reduction in the prostate-specific antigen level. During the 35-month follow-up after re-salvage focal low-dose rate brachytherapy, no recurrence of prostate cancer and no severe radiation-related toxicities were observed.
