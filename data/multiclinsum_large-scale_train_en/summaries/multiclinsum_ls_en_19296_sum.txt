We here report a case of tracheal stenosis with a delayed manifestation, presenting 35 years after endotracheal intubation for neonatal resuscitation. A female patient complained of dyspnea during pregnancy. Bronchoscopy revealed 75% constriction of the tracheal lumen by cicatrization, from the 2nd to 4th tracheal rings. After child-birth, the scar tissue was ablated using argon plasma coagulation.
