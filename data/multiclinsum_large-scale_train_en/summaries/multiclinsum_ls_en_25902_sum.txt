A 66-year-old woman with septic shock due to left obstructive pyelonephritis was transferred to our hospital. Tazobactam/Piperacillin 4.5 g was administered every 8 h for 5 days. The patient's condition improved, and she was transferred back to the previous hospital. Kluyvera intermedia was obtained by blood cultures. The patient was successfully treated with a two-week course of antibiotics.
