The case of a 35-year-old pregnant woman with a 10-year history of mild mesangial proliferative glomerulonephritis is described here. CKD progressed from stage 3 to stage 5 rapidly during pregnancy, and protective hemodialysis was started at 28 wk of gestation. Due to preeclampsia at 34 wk of gestation, cesarean section was performed and a healthy baby was delivered. Hemodialysis was discontinued at 4 wk postpartum. After 1 year of follow-up, her renal function was stable, and her baby exhibited good growth and development.
