A 77-year-old man previously visited a doctor because an echo scan unexpectedly revealed an abdominal tumor. Contrast computed tomography (CT) disclosed a large multilocular cystic tumor spanning from the left upper abdomen to the retroperitoneum, and poorly marginated soft tissue structures were present around the abdominal aorta, inferior vena cava, pancreas, mesentery, and left kidney.
