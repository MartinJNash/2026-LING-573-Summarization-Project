We report a 13-year-old Chinese adolescent with absent Müllerian derivatives and suspected testis in the inguinal area. History, examinations, and assistant examinations were available for clinical diagnosis of 46,XY DSD. The subsequent targeting specific disease-causing genes, comprising 360 endocrine disease-causing genes, was employed for molecular diagnosis.
