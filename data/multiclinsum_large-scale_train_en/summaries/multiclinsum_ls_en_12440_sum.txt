A previously healthy 34-year-old G2P1 at 30 weeks gestation presented to the emergency department with shortness of breath and chest pain. She was diagnosed with PE and subsequently required intensive care unit (ICU) admission and thrombolysis for a massive PE. While on a therapeutic dose of tinzaparin she had recurrence of PE in the post-partum period. She was treated with supratherapeutic tinzaparin and subsequently transitioned to warfarin. She was found to have a PVA and ultimately underwent successful PVA ligation. She remains on anticoagulation for secondary prevention of VTE.
