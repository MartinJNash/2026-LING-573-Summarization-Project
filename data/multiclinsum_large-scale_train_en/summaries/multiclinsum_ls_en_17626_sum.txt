A female presented with a history of JAK2V617F positive PV, diagnosed in 2007, and right adrenal pheochromocytoma diagnosed and resected in 2011. Her polycythemia symptoms and hematocrit levels continued to worsen from 2007 to 2011, with an increased frequency of phlebotomies. Postoperatively, until early 2013, her hematocrit levels remained normalized. Following this, the hematocrit levels ranged between 46.4 and 48.9%
