We report a case of a giant ABC in the distal tibia one-third with soft tissue component of a 30-year-old male. The patient presented to our outpatient department with complaints of pain and swelling over left ankle for 1 year. The size of the swelling was 15 cm × 10 cm × 10 cm over medial aspect of ankle with 3 discharging sinuses which present over swelling. His blood parameters were suggestive of low hemoglobin count. X-rays showed cystic lesions over medial aspect of left ankle. Computed tomography scan and magnetic resonance imaging reports were suggestive of ABC.
