We describe the case of a previously unreported low-grade fibromyxoid tumor of the cerebellum in an 8-year-old male. Extensive immunohistochemical, next-generation sequencing, and attempted DNA methylation profiling are reported. There has been no recurrence during the 6-year follow-up. Screening excluded multiple myxoid tumors, including low-grade fibromyxoid sarcoma. The findings suggest that, with gross total resection, the lesions may not recur.
