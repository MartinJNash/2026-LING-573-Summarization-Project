A 52-year-old man was diagnosed with sigmoid colon cancer and polyp, and was scheduled for laparoscopic sigmoidectomy. On the day before operation, fluorescent clips (ZEOCLIP FS: Zeon Medical co, Ltd, Tokyo) were endoscopically placed around the tumor and polyp sites, 4 clips for each lesion attached every 90 degrees within the colonic lumen, respectively. During the operation, locations of the fluorescent clips were easily confirmed using a full-color fluorescent laparoscope, VISION SENSE (Medtronic Co., U.S.). Curative operation was performed accordingly with the preoperative pathological and radiological findings. The postoperative course was uneventful.
