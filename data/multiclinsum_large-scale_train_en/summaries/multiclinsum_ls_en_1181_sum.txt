Here, we present an unusual case of focal hematopoietic hyperplasia of the rib who arrived to our institute with a complaint of severing back pain at the level of T5-T6 vertebrae. The diagnosis of FHH has been made based on clinical and radiologic findings and confirmed by histopathologic evaluations. We also reviewed all previously reported cases of Focal Hematopoietic hyperplasia of the rib in the literature.
