A 50-year-old with late presentation of palmar dislocation of the fifth metacarpal bone, the diagnosis that went unnoticed in the initial emergency assessment. The patient was taken to an open reduction and internal fixation with an internal brace fixed at the base of the fifth metacarpal, the base of the fourth metacarpal and the hamate, and the fifth metacarpal was fixed to the fourth metacarpal with a Kirschner wire placed transversely to the axis of the metacarpal.
