A 66-year-old man presented with bilateral direct inguinal hernia and enlargement of the prostate. Prostate biopsy shows atypical adenomatous hyperplasia accompanied by chronic inflammatory cells. Surgery was performed with laparoscopic TEP by using mesh and TURP. The patient underwent a combination of laparoscopic TEP and TURP. A single preoperative and postoperative dose of levofloxacin was administered. The laparoscopic TEP and TURP procedures were completed with no difficulty. Hematoma at the right inguinal occurred three weeks after the procedure. Bilateral inguinal abscess and sepsis occurred at 3-month follow-up. We performed laparotomy mesh evacuation and debridement, and found no recurrence of the hernia.
