A 23-year-old Thai male came to our hospital with left-sided nasal obstruction. Endoscopic examination found a mass originated from the left sphenoethmoidal recess. Incisional biopsy result of the mass indicated an inflammatory process and high level of serum specific IgE to several aeroallergens was found. Based on the biopsy results and other investigations, the diagnosis of AFRS was made and the patient was treated successfully with endoscopic sinus surgery and postoperative systemic/topical steroids.
