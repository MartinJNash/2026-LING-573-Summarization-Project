A 25-year-old male presented 4 days after a motorbike accident. He was diagnosed as a femoral head fracture with neck fracture of the left side. This fracture is classified as type III Pipkin fracture or OTA type 31C.3. This case was managed with open reduction and internal fixation utilizing the trochanteric flip osteotomy and multiple screws. It is worth to report the first case of Pipkin type three fracture having satisfactory functional outcome after 5 years of follow-up.
