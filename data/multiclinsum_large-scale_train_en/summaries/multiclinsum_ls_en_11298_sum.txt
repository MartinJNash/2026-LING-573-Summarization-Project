The authors report a case of a female 65-year-old suffering from end-stage kidney failure for 10 years, who presented with a double left renal tumor, composed by an oncocytoma associated to multiple CCPRCC, a very rare entity. A radical left nephrectomy was realized by lumbotomy, with an uneventful postoperative course. Histological examination was challenging. Immunohistological examination showed diffuse positivity of cytokertain 7. No local recurrence nor metastatic progression were found during the 12 months of follow-up.
