A two-year-old boy presented with scoliosis deformity. The mother noticed the patient's deformity when he was 10th month of age as he learned to stand and progressed since then. There were no growth and development problems. On physical examination, the patient appeared to have scoliosis at lumbar level with bending to the right and asymmetry of waist fold with left shoulder depression. Based on X-ray and CT-Scan investigations, the patient was diagnosed with single fully segmented hemivertebra at 3rd lumbar level. 20 × 106 UC-MSCs were implanted into the bone defect of hemivertebra.
