A 67-year-old male farmer was referred to our hospital with a 6-day history of fever and shortness of breath. The radiologic findings revealed a dense consolidation site in the right lung, considering infection lesion, accompanying with mediastinal and right hilar lymphadenopathy. Sputum sample results showed P. aeruginosa and multidrug-resistant Acinetobacter baumannii. The combination of polymyxin B and amikacin was given to him based on the lab results, the manifestations of the patient were alleviated and he returned to his home after a 35-day hospitalization.
