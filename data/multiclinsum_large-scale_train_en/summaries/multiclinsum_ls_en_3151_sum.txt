The patient was a 49-year-old female, she had a pain mass on the right mandible a year ago and was treated with root canal therapy and marginal resection. After seven months, the mass recurred and enlarged. Enhanced CT scan, laboratory examination, Ultrasonography, 99mTc-MIBI SPECT-CT scintiscan and pathological examination were used to confirm the diagnosis of brown tumor. The patient's symptom improved after parathyroidectomy.
