We present a case of 70 year old female admitted with chief complaints of right iliac fossa pain intermittent in nature with nausea and generalized weakness since 1 month. With the help of USG and CECT preoperative diagnosis of Appendiceal Mucocele was made. Midline incision Laparotomy was performed. Preoperative diagnosis of Appendix Mucocele changing the course of management has been reported rarely in literature.
