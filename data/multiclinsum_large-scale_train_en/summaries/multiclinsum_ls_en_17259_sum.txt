A 69-year-old female presented at our hospital with a growing mass on the right side of her neck. An elastic, soft mass measuring 9 cm × 6 cm was palpable in the right cervical region. Laboratory analyses showed pancytopenia, increased serum lactate dehydrogenase levels, and markedly increased levels of soluble interleukin-2 receptor. Enhanced computed tomography (CT) and fluorodeoxyglucose positron emission tomography (PET)/CT revealed multiple lesions throughout her body.
