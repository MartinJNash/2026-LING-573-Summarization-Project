A 66-year-old female presented with constipation and abdominal pain. She was found to have an obstructing sigmoid colon lesion, multiple metastatic lesions in the liver, and a pancreatic tail lesion. She underwent left hemicolectomy and ostomy placement. The gross pathology of the colon and needle biopsy of the liver was consistent of pancreatobiliary origin. Genomic screening performed, patient found to be KRAS G12R mutated. She was given one cycle of chemotherapy, thereafter was referred to hospice care.
