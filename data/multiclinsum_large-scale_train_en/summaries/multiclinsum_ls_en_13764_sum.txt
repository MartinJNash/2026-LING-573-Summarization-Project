A 52-year-old Thai female without preexisting shoulder pathology and symptom had developed left shoulder pain for 3 days. Two weeks prior to shoulder pain, she received an mRNA COVID-19 vaccination. She positioned her arm in combined internal rotation with 60° of arm abduction. Her symptoms were pain in all directions of shoulder motion and tenderness at the bicipital groove and over the deltoid area. The testing of the rotator cuff power of the infraspinatus tendon showed pain.
