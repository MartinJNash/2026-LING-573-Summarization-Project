TIVITA ® is a viable tool for the identification of segments with reduced StO2 (inflow occlusion: 0.23 ± 0.03 vs normal: 0.50 ± 0.06) and NIR Perfusion (inflow occlusion: 0.02 ± 0.04 vs normal: 0.47 ± 0.06) and allows for a visual differentiation of well oxygenated, perfused (green) and low oxygenated, poorly perfused (blue) liver tissue in a patient undergoing left hemihepatectomy for hepatocellular carcinoma.
