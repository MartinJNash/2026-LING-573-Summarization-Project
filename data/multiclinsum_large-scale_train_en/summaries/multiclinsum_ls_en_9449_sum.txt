In this case, we reported that a patient with preeclampsia was diagnosed with HELLP syndrome. Furthermore, hepatic infarction also was found via the computed tomographic (CT) images, which showed peripheral wedge-shaped inhomogeneous low attenuation in the right hepatic lobes via plain CT scan, and the low-density shadow and mottled appearance in the same areas where vessels were seen coursing through them via contrast-enhanced CT scan.
