A young female patient presented with a slowly growing mass at the submandibular area and swelling in the floor of mouth. The MRI showed large sublingual cyst extended posteriorly to the parapharyngeal space. She underwent surgical excision of the cyst size 6.5 × 3.2 × 2.5 cm via an intraoral approach. The pathological examination revealed a benign stratified squamous epithelium-lined cyst with no evidence of adnexal structure consistent with an epidermoid cyst. The patient remained disease-free after two years of follow-up.
