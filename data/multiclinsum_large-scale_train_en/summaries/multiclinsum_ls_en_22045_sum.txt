A 71-year-old man was found unconscious after taking a 10-min bath and brought to the emergency room. The head computed tomography (HCT) was normal, and he was diagnosed with heatstroke as a complication of influenza A. Despite effective therapy to correct his temperature, his consciousness did not improve, and within 24 h he progressed to multiple organ injury. Repeat HCT and subsequent magnetic resonance imaging revealed irreparably progressed ANE.
