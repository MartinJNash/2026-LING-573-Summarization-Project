Herein is reported the case of a 36-year-old woman who presented with a hard nodular dark-bluish umbilicus mass that had a tendency to increase in size and was accompanied by severe pain during menstruation for ∼2 years is reported. Laparotomy revealed a normal uterus without involvement of any other pelvic organ by the endometrial tissue except the umbilicus part. Histological evaluation revealed endometriosis of the umbilicus.
