In this article we report three years old male patient with incomplete urethral duplication and epispadias. During our examination there was detected the second - accessory urethra along with the main urethra on the dorsal surface of the penile. The accessory urethra was stripped from the surrounding tissues to the root of the penile and it became apparent that the accessory urethra was merged into the main urethra at the same level. The accessory urethra was closed by suturing and removed from that part. Then, by using 7.0 pds thread there was performed urethroplasty.
