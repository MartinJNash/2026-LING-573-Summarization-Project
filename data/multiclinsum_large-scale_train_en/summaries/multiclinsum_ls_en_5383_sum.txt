A 29-year-old female, a known case of hypothyroidism and adjustment disorder, under levothyroxine, propranolol and sertraline, intentionally ingested 2.5 mg of levothyroxine but remained asymptomatic with sudden surge in T4 in initial hours of ingestion which gradually started declining along with reciprocal change in TSH. However, the change in T3 was almost negligible.
