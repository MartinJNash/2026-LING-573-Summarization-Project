A 31-year-old male with relapsing-remitting multiple sclerosis developed macular edema and retinal hemorrhages in his left eye, 1 month after starting fingolimod treatment; treatment was then discontinued. The hemorrhages were flame-shaped, and were extensive along retinal arteries and veins. The hemorrhages started to decrease at 4 weeks and disappeared completely at 24 weeks after cessation of fingolimod treatment.
