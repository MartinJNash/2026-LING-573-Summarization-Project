We are reporting a case of 62 years female who underwent fixation of an intertrochanteric fracture of femur using a dynamic hip screw (DHS) and subsequently started developing ischemic changes in the operated lower limb on the 3rd post-operative day. Angiography revealed thrombosis of the superficial femoral artery at the level of the third screw of the DHS and the patient ultimately landed up with an amputation.
