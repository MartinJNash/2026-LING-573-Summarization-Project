A girl was admitted to our hospital due to SARS-CoV-2 infection, 3 months before admission the patient received LT due to Niemann-Pick disease (NPD). During hospitalization, blood gas analysis showed severe mixed acidosis. To relieve mixed acidosis, the patient was given oral rehydration salt and liquid replacement therapy. Considering that immunosuppressants may cause metabolic acidosis, dose of CsA was decreased and MMF was discontinued.
