This case report is to discuss a 23 years old female patient with a neglected stent after right pyelolithotomy was done 6 years back. The presence of the stent was identified incidentally after she visited local health facility for recurrent LUTS. The CT scan shows fragmented and encrusted stent with in the bladder and pelvis, stones in stent coils and isolated lower pole stone. She was managed by a procedure of cystolithotomy and right PCNL in separate sessions.
