A 5-year-old Iranian boy was diagnosed with a right inguinal hernia. He underwent right inguinal herniorrhaphy at the age of 1 year. For this case report, the hernia symptoms had returned. Both testicles were palpated in the right scrotum, an ultrasound examination also revealed both testicles to be present in the right scrotum, and a hernia sac located in the right inguinal region with an internal ring. The patient was recommended to undergo a surgical reconstruction. Surgical reconstruction was performed by crossing the left testis in the transseptal orchiopexy technique.
