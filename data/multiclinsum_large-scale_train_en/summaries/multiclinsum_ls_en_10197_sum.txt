We present a case of prolonged SARS-CoV-2 infection in a patient who had undergone B-cell depletion therapy. Chest computed tomography (CT) revealed notable migratory pulmonary infiltrates, and bronchoalveolar lavage fluid was positive for SARS-CoV-2 (day-120). The patient responded well to the combination treatment of antiviral medication, convalescent plasma, and corticosteroid.
