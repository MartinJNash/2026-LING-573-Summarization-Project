A 65-year-old woman who underwent distal gastrectomy for gastric cancer a year earlier had a splenic tumor that was increasing in size. We planned laparoscopic splenectomy because there was a possibility of a malignant splenic tumor. Intraoperative ICG fluorescence imaging confirmed perfusion of the remnant stomach. The patient was discharged on postoperative day 8 after an uncomplicated postoperative course.
