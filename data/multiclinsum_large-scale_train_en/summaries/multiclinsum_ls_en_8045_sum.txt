A 50-year-old woman had a left paraclinoid aneurysm with a maximum diameter of 6.1 mm. We planned FRED deployment. We experienced in-stent stenosis just after the deployment of a FRED. Devices such as guidewires and catheters could not cross the lesion through the FRED because of an obstruction in the FRED. Balloon angioplasty and subsequent stenting resolved thrombosis and kinking. FRED has potential for kinking locally.
