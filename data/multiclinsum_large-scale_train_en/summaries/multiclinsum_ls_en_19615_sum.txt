A 49-year-old woman with a left adrenal tumor, lymph node metastasis around the aorta, and multiple liver metastases was treated with mitotane. The disease progressed despite mitotane therapy; thus, combination chemotherapy with paclitaxel and carboplatin plus mitotane was administered for 9 months. Primary adrenal resection was performed after the liver metastasis had completely dissapeared. She has remained alive for 20 years since her initial diagnosis while undergoing mitotane therapy.
