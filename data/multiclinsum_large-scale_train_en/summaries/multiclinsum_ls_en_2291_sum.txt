A 30-year old, nulliparous virgin woman admitted to the outpatient- clinic with the complaint of irregular menstrual bleeding ongoing for three months. Pelvic ultrasound revealed a 5×6 cm solid mass in the cervico-vaginal location that filled the vaginal margins. Due to the patient's consistent desire for preserving hymenal integrity, mini-laparotomic colpotomy was performed and the mass was removed successfully.
