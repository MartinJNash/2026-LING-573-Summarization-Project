A 32-year-old gravida III para II Amhara woman presented with shortness of breath and progressive abdominal distension and pain. An emergency laparotomy was performed with the impression of abdominal pregnancy. Intraoperatively, the fetus was seen in an intact amniotic sac in her abdomen, her uterus was ruptured at the fundus and the placenta was extensively adhered to the edge of the ruptured site. The patient and neonate progressed well and were discharged.
