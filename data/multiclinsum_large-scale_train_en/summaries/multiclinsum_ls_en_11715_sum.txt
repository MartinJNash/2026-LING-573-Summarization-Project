A 56-year-old Kuwaiti woman with hepatitis C virus infection was treated with pegylated interferon α-2a and ribavirin, laboratory test results prior to therapy were within normal values. After 4 weeks of that treatment, she developed neutropenia and severe thrombocytopenia. Her hepatitis C virus treatment was stopped for many years until eltrombopag was used as an off-label drug with an episode of severe thrombocytopenia. Her platelets count returned to normal level when triple therapy for hepatitis C virus was used successfully.
