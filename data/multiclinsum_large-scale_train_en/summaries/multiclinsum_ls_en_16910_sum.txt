We report the case of a 64-year-old active male who had previously underwent a subacromial balloon placement in his right shoulder and an arthroscopic rotator cuff repair procedure on his left shoulder. He later presented with persistent pain and disability in his left shoulder and opted to undergo a second subacromial balloon placement on his left side. To the best of our knowledge, this is the first case of bilateral subacromial balloon placement procedure in the literature.
