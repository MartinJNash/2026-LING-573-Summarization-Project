We report a rare case in which the CEA level continued to increase and the Ctn value was normal after total thyroidectomy and central lymph node dissection in a MTC patient. The patient was asymptomatic during one and half year follow-up until lateral lymph node metastasis was revealed. However, the CEA level raised again after lateral neck lymph node dissection and bone metastases were found by 18F-FDG PET-CT.
