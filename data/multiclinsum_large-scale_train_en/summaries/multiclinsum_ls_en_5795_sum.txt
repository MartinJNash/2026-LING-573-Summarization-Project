A Chinese 58-year-old female renal insufficient patient with no history of smoking was diagnosed as stage IIIA (T2N2M0) lung adenocarcinoma. Next-generation sequencing targeting 520 cancer-related genes was performed on the pleural effusion samples and revealed 2 novel RET fusions LINCO1264-RET and SEMA5A-RET, concomitant with a common CCDC6-RET.
