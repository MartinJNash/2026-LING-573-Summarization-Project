A 61-year-old male patient with congenital MGA, who had been treated with the Morrow procedure for HCM, had worsening symptoms in recent years that were difficult to control medically. He was diagnosed with occult obstructive HCM by stress echocardiography. After multi-disciplinary discussion, this patient was treated with PIMSRA. The post-operative clinical outcome was remarkable, with a significant decrease in septal thickness and disappearance of the left anterior branch conduction block.
