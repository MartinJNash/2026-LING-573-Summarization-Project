This report presents the case of a newborn with cCMV infection with diffuse parenchymal calcifications, white matter (WM) abnormalities and cerebellar hypoplasia on postnatal brain imaging after magnetic resonance imaging (MRI) and neurosonogram (NSG) at 30 weeks showing lenticulostriate vasculopathy, bilateral temporal cysts and normal gyration pattern according to the gestational age (GA). No calcifications were seen on prenatal imaging.
