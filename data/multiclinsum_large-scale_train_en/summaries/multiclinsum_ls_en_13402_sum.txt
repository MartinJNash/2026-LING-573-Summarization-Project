In this case series, we recruited 7 patients undergoing in vitro fertilisation (IVF) treatment previously diagnosed with TB between January 01, 2014 and June 30, 2021. Patients were recruited at the beginning of their IVF treatments. Of 7 patients, 2 patients (28.6%) achieved live birth. 5 patients (71.4%) failed to conceive. All patients had good and/or excellent quality embryos upon transfer but only 2/7 managed to conceive and delivered.
