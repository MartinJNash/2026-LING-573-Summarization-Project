We report in this study a case of Essential Progressive Iris Atrophy, an Iridocorneal Endothelial Syndrome variant in a 40 years old patient, female, complaining about the shape of the pupil in the left eye, as well as photophobia in the same side.In the first evaluation, we observed visual acuity of 1.0 in both eyes.Intraocular pressure was 14 mm Hg in the right eye and 12 mm Hg in the left eye. On the biomicroscope, we had a proper right eye finding, on the left eye Iris atrophy with deformity in the direction from 12 to 6 hours. We performed gonioscopy, an ultra sound (UBM), spectral microscopy, pachymetry, OCT and Octopus perimetry.
