We recently treated a 30-year-old Japanese female who achieved molecular remission with only the administration of all-trans retinoic acid. The patient's leukemic clones concomitantly had a del(5q) aberrant chromosome with t(15;17) (q22;q12). The patient's bone marrow cells indicated clonal evolution of the tumor cells expressing CD13dim, CD33+, CD117+, and lacking HLA-DR, CD34 and CD11b. A fluorescence in situ hybridization analysis detected PML-RARA fusion genes in the patient's bone marrow specimens, leading to the diagnosis of acute promyelocytic leukemia.
