A 48-year-old male patient presented with a 2-year history of lower back pain and numbness in both lower extremities, which has progressively worsened over the past 3 months. An Magnetic resonance imaging scan revealed an oval-shaped lesion located in the L2/3 vertebral segment, which migrated to the L3/4 vertebral segment following contrast enhancement. The patient underwent tumor resection surgery, and the diagnosis of schwannoma was confirmed through pathological examination.
