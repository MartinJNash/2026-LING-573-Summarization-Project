The patient visited our clinic due to non-nephrotic range proteinuria for 3 months, but no obvious abnormality was detected in the vital signs or laboratory test results. Renal histopathology revealed mitochondrial nephropathy, which manifested as mild glomerular abnormalities under light microscope, together with mitochondrial proliferation and hypertrophy and crowded arrangement under electron microscope. As suggested by whole exome sequencing, the patient inherited the COQ8B compound heterozygous variants from both of his parents who showed normal phenotype.
