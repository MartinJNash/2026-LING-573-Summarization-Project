The case presents a 63-year-old female having MR findings such as hemorraged supratentorial dural-based mass with "popcorn" signs and hemosiderin deposits. The patient underwent craniotomy and evacuation of the subdural hematoma with resection of the tumor mass. Histologically the lesion had signs of a cavernoma and meningioma. MRI in a year after surgery did not reveale residual tumor mass or recurrence.
