We Report a 24 year-old female patient with a Pathological Fracture of the Tibia in a Monostotic Anterior Cortical Fibrous Dysplasia Hybrid Fixation with a UnicorticalOsteoperisoteal Non Vascularized Fibula Graft with a follow up until the union of fracture site on computed tomography scan and complete integration of unicorticalosteoperiosteal fibular graft .
