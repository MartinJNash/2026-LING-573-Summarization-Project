A 16-year-old immunocompromised girl presented with fever and a non-productive cough. A chest computed tomography (CT) scan revealed diffuse pulmonary nodules with cavities. Subsequent results were consistent with disseminated cryptococcosis with Cryptococcus identified in her blood, bone marrow and cerebrospinal fluid cultures. Thus, the patient was diagnosed with disseminated cryptococcal infection with PC, cryptococcus meningitis, cryptococcus osteomyelitis and cryptococcus sepsis. After antifungal treatment, the patient demonstrated both clinical and chest radiological improvement.
