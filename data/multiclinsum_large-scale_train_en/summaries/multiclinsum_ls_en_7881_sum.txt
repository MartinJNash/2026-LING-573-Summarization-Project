We experienced a case who had alcoholic ketoacidosis and severe hypoglycemia after drinking too much alcohol without taking enough food for a long period of time. In this subject, plasma glucose level was as low as 25 mg/dL, and ketone bodies, especially 3-hydroxybutyrate, were markedly increased. In addition, in blood gas analysis, severe acidosis was observed and anion gap was increased. These points were compatible with hypoglycemia alcoholic ketoacidosis.
