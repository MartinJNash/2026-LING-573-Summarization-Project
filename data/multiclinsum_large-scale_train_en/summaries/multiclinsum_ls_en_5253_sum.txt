We describe a 12-year-old male proband originating from Al-Quneitra province - southwest Syria. Hematological investigations revealed, pallor and anemia (Hb 9 g/dl). The mean cell volume (MCV) 64 fL; mean cell hemoglobin (MCH) 21.8 pg. Capillary electrophoresis (CE) electropherogram revealed low level of Hb A1 (36.2%), high level of Hb F (62.2%) and low level of Hb A2 (1.6%). The proband requires blood transfusion occasionally. Direct DNA sequencing and Polymerase chain reaction-restriction fragment length polymorphism (PCR/RFLP) for mutations detection were used. The molecular analysis revealed the presence of rare β+ Hb Knossos codon 27 (G > T) (HBB: c.82G > T) variant associated with β0 codon 5
