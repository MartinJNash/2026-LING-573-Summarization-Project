We present a case where a SAP patient presenting with a sudden hyperpyrexia was treated for 19 days. We monitored her routine blood panel and CRP levels once or twice daily. The results showed that WBC count decreased gradually. And the lowest level of WBC was appeared at the 21st day of treatment. WBC 0.58 × 109/L(4.0-10.0 × 109/L), neutrophils 0.1 × 109/L
