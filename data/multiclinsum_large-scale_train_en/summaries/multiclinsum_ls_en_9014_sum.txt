A 40-year-old female operated and diagnosed case of the right parietal anaplastic astrocytoma presented to us with a recurrence in parietal region. In addition, a fresh lesion was observed in the right MTL suggestive of a separate temporal glioma. The patient underwent excision of both parietal and temporal gliomas through the parietal approach only. Complete excision of parietal recurrence and near-total excision of medial temporal glioma was achieved.
