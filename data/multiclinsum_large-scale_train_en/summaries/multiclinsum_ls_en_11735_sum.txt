A 58-year-old Japanese man visited us because of a soft tissue mass on the lateral aspect of the left shoulder that had been noticed 2 years prior. The spherical tumor, which measured 5 cm × 4 cm, was elastic and firm on palpation and immobile. Magnetic resonance imaging revealed that the lesion was localized in the left deltoid muscle. A needle biopsy was performed to make a histological diagnosis. With a pre-operative diagnosis of intramuscular lipoma, we removed the tumor with the patient under general anesthesia. The tumor was removed with surrounding musculature and fascia. The pathological diagnosis was intramuscular spindle cell lipoma in the left deltoid muscle.
