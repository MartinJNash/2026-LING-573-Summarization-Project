We recruited a 25-year-old male volunteer who was able to have male squirting. A transrectal ultrasound probe was inserted into the rectum and male squirting was observed following normal ejaculation. With penis stimulation for a further 20 s after ejaculation, translucent misty fluids with a creatine level similar to that of urine came from the extraurethral orifice for about 60 s. Color Doppler ultrasonography recorded strong contraction of the prostate and pelvic striated muscles just before male squirting, and then the stream went from the urinary bladder through the prostatic urethra.
