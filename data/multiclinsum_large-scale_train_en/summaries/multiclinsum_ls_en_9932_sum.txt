In this report, a 6 years old girl was presented who was referred to the Pediatric Dep, Shahid Sadoughi Hospital,Yazd, Iran, due to multiple congenital anomalies such as: frontal bossing, horizontal palpebral fissure, small deepest eyes, aplastic nasal bridge, broad philtrum, low set ears, large prominent ears, short neck, microcephaly, pectus excavatum, mental retardation, and dislocation of the hip. In peripheral blood smear, platelets were decreased but other hematological levels were normal. The karyotype result indicated a mosaic monosomy and partial monosomy of chromosome 21.
