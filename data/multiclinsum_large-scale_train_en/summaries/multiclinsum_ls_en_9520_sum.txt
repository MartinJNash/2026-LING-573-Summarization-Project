We described a male patient had a 4-year history of recurrent fatigue. Serum biochemistry revealed hypokalemia with renal potassium wasting, hypomagnesemia, metabolic alkalosis, hyperreninemia, hypocalciuria, as well as nephrotic-range proteinuria, hypoalbuminemia, and elevated serum anti-phospholipase A2 receptor (PLA2R) antibody. Gene sequencing identified compound heterozygous mutations in SLC12A3
