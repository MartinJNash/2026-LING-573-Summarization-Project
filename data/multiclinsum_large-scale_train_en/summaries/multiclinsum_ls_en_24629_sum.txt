We presented a case of a 71-year-old woman who was diagnosed with bilateral knee osteoarthritis with varus deformity, especially on the right side. After admission, the right total knee arthroplasty was performed with the assistance of MR technology. Before the operation, the three-dimensional virtual model of the knee joint of the patient was reconstructed for condition analysis, operation plan formulation, and operation simulation. During the operation, the three-dimensional virtual images of the femur and tibia coincided with the real body of the patient, showing the osteotomy plane designed before the operation, which can accurately guide the completion of osteotomy and prosthesis implantation.
