In the presenting case, we reported a 26-year-old male patient with no known history of chronic disease who presented to the emergency department with a complaint of 1-week generalized edema, nausea, fatigue, and generalized ache in the extremities. He was diagnosed with NS complicated by hypothyroidism and was hospitalized for 3 weeks. After 3 weeks of treatment and close monitoring, the patient's clinical condition and laboratory investigations were improved, and was discharged in good health.
