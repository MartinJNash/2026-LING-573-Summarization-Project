We present a 16 year-old male who developed quotidian fever, headache, conjunctival injection, sore throat, nausea and vomiting, diarrhea, rash, and symmetrical polyarticular arthralgia/arthritis 4 weeks after exposure to SARS-CoV-2 and 2 weeks after his first vaccination against COVID-19. Our patient's laboratory results were significant for elevated inflammatory markers and acute phase reactants. He met criteria for diagnosis with both MIS-C and AOSD. After receiving first-line treatment for both diseases, IVIG and methylprednisolone, our patient improved.
