A 38-year-old woman, 5G/5P, with no pathological history, who presented 2 days postpartum with no notion of instrumental extraction, with severe pelvic symphysis pain with functional impotence. Examination revealed elective pain on palpation of the pubic symphysis without radiation. X-ray of the pelvis showed a 15-mm enlargement of the symphysis. CT scan of the pelvis showed a 17-mm disjunction of the pubic symphysis. The patient received analgesic treatment with pelvic bandage and anticoagulant therapy with good clinical response.
