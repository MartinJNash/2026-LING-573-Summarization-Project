We report a 61 year old Hispanic male who had an EC, adenocarcinoma subtype, with UM to an extensive number of sites such as the soft tissue of the cheek, subcutaneous forehead and scalp with penetration of the calvarium, occipital bone, base of tongue, neck muscles, paraspinal and iliopsoas musculature and, more typically, to an adrenal gland followed by a questionable new-onset finding of subcutaneous shoulder and flank nodules not initially identified by imaging.
