We report a case of a 46-year-old male suffering from GC with PD-L1 negative BrMs 12 years after GC resection and 5 cycles of chemotherapy. We treated the patient with the immune checkpoint inhibitor (ICI) pembrolizumab and all metastatic tumors achieved a complete response (CR). A durable remission of the tumors is confirmed after 4 years of follow-up.
