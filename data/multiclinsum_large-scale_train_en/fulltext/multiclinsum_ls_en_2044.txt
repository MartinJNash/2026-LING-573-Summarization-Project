A 24-year old man who suffered a motorcycle accident was referred to Shahid Kamyab Hospital. An examination of the subject revealed an avulsion of his periorbital skin that was 2 × 3 cm in size . The patient had no medical history.
To reconstruct the avulsed area a PRF membrane was made using 10 cc of blood obtained from the brachial vein. PRF was obtained following centrifugation of the blood sample. The membrane was then made and placed on the area of avulsion to reduce the amount of scar tissue following the healing process. The membrane was sutured in place with 5-0 Nylon suture material .
After 8 weeks the subject demonstrated uneven healing of the area with a small amount of scar tissue .
