A 57-year old female, nonsmoker with a mass in left lung revealed by routine physical examination. CT scan revealed a 3 × 3 × 3 cm mass located at the dorsal segment of left lower lobe and projected into the lobe bronchus. Adenocarcinoma was diagnosed by bronchoscope examination . The left main and upper lobe bronchus were not involved. The pulmonary artery and vein were free of tumor. Before the operation, the pulmonary function test, blood gas analysis, cardiac evaluation and basic examinations showed normal with no other comorbidities. The operation was performed using the Da Vinci surgical system by Dr. Jiao in Jan 29, 2015.
