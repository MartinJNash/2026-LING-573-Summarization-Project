In September 2020, a 66-year-old woman was admitted to our hospital for progressive chest tightness and shortness of breath.
One year prior, the patient had been admitted to the hospital with severe anaemia and thrombocytosis. Physical examination showed that the patient had an anaemic appearance. The results of peripheral blood examination were as follows: red blood cell (RBC) count, 1.0 × 1012/L; platelet (PLT) count, 409 × 109/L; haemoglobin (HB), 36 g/L; creatinine, 0.55 mg/dL; and lactate dehydrogenase (LDH), 418 U/L. Bone marrow trephine biopsy revealed more than 10% abnormal megakaryocytes (single round nuclei and cytosolic lobulated micronuclei) . Fluorescence in situ hybridization (FISH) indicated deletion of the EGR1 (5q31) gene . Furthermore, the karyotype was described as 46, XX, del(5)(q13q31) by G band staining . Based on clinical manifestations and laboratory tests, the patient was diagnosed with low-risk MDS (low risk, IPSS-R = 2.5). The endothelial activation and stress index (EASIX) was 0.56. EASIX is an independent prognostic factor for lower-risk MDS patents that was calculated by the following formula: LDH (U/L) × creatinine (mg/dL)/PLT (nL). The patient was advised to be treated with lenalidomide. The dosing schedule was 10 mg/d or 21 d in a 28-d cycle. After three cycles of treatment, peripheral blood examination showed HB 97 g/L and PLT 341 × 109/L.
The patient was previously healthy. There was no disease history in other systems.
No contributory personal history or similar family history.
Physical examination showed that the patient had a moderate anaemic appearance. Her vital signs were stable, with no other positive findings.
The results of the peripheral blood examination were as follows: RBC, 2.27 × 1012/L; WBC, 1.93 × 109/L; PLT, 114 × 109/L; and HB, 58 g/L.
Bone marrow aspirate smears showed hypercellularity with marked myeloid and erythroid hypoplasia and a blast cell count of 16% . Another karyotype examination revealed 46, XX, inv(3)(q21q26), and del(5)(q13q31) . qRT–PCR showed that the EVI1 expression level was 90.63%, which was classified as high expression.
